What is QUAITOM?

QUAITOM is an initiative to bring 3D imaging to the attention of the AI research field. We will create volumetric 3D data sets that can be used for benchmarking deep learning algorithms, which are beyond traditional clinical data. Segmentation is a major task for 3D imaging research, and we will therefore focus on data for testing segmentation methods. By choosing and annotating the data in a way that reflects the typical analysis pipeline used for 3D imaging, we hope to get the deep learning community interested, and get deep learning methods and approaches developed that are useful for the 3D imaging research community.
